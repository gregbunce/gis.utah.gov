Vintage

The Utah Geological Survey created digital 7.5, 15 and 30-minute vintage U.S. Geological Survey topographic quadrangle maps (1900-1966) by scanning original paper maps at a resolution of 500 dots per inch (dpi). These digital scans were enhanced in Adobe Photoshop as needed. Subsequent 16 point spline georeferencing was done in ArcMap. There is not a statewide collection of these vintage topographic maps and they have a NAD27 Datumn.
