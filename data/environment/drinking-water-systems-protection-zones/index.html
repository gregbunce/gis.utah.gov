Monitored Lakes

Environment.DWQMonitoredLakes132 contains locations of the 132 lakes in Utah monitored for water quality by the Division of Water Quality (Department of Environmental Quality). Environment.LakeMonitoring contains 729 Monitored Lakes. Each location is a point, which is located within the lake's high water level shoreline.
