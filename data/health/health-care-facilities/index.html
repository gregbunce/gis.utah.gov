Emergency Medical Services

Health.EmergencyMedicalServices represents the Emergency Medical Service (EMS) Facilities throughout the state of Utah. An EMS Facility is any location where emergency medical services personnel are stationed or based out of, or where EMS equipment is stored and ready use. Health.EmergencyMedicalServices includes both private and governmental entities. The NAME field contains the name of the EMS Facility.
